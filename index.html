1st Monthsary

Our neediness help each other to keep us on going forward and look to a new future. We may be an overthinker but our minds does'nt connect our own worlds together, it always overlap from each other. However this does'nt stop us from meeting each other in its epicenter.

I consider you the moon of my dark world and even if the new sun emerge it will never fades away.
